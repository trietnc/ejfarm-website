Our beloved Dak Nong

"How it all began"

EJ Farm celebrates the land, the people, and the traditions behind our coffee. Working with small, family-run farms across the region, we partner directly with producers to craft single-origin coffees that reflect place, care, and time.

From seed to cup, every step is guided by respect for the land and the communities who tend it. Our goal is to share those stories - the hands, the moments, and the flavors that make each harvest distinctive.
