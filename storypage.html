The Soul of Đắk Nông

This is not just a farm. It is a 20-year-old promise, rooted in the volcanic Bazan soil of Vietnam's highlands. This is the story of a land and its people, on a journey to share their purest craft with the world.

The Path We Pursue

Con Đường Theo Đuổi

2014

A Heritage Discovered

Our story begins with the discovery of a 20-year-old farm in Mơ Nông. Here, the land was already rich, the coffee plants mature, and the volcanic Bazan soil was producing beans of exceptional, untapped quality. We saw not just a farm, but a legacy.

2016

The Purity Mission

In a market full of blended and impure coffee, our mission was clear: let the land speak for itself. We committed to 100% pure, single-origin coffee. This path was harder, but it was the only way to honor the true, rich flavor of Đắk Nông.

2019

A Prosperous Community

Cộng Đồng Thịnh Vượng

This land is tended by its people. We built our mission around the hard-working farming community, creating a sustainable model that ensures their passion and expertise are rewarded. Our strength comes from this collective effort.

Today

The Ambitious Goal

Mục Tiêu Xa

Our journey is far from over. Today, our goal is to bring this story—this taste of pure Vietnamese terroir—to the world. We are proving that a small, passionate farm from Đắk Nông can set a new global standard for quality and purity.

Taste the Journey

Our story is in every bean. Experience the pure, unblended, and ambitious flavor of Đắk Nông.
